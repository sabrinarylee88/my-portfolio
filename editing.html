I started to understanding editing better when I taught writing to ESL college students, primarily Mandarin speakers.

It felt so intimate to sit next to someone, toy with their thoughts and doubts while helping them find the words to express them. I felt lucky to have these exchanges.

I was surprised to find that being that the most difficult thing about editing had nothing to do with writing or language at all.

It was about managing the writing process while being patient with it and putting aside your own instincts in service of someone else's vision.
