I'm Sabrina, a writer based in Singapore. I've been thinking about how I want to present my work in some kind of presentable way. However, I think what I needed more was a space to experiment and a space that felt exciting to come back to and work in. So here's my brain dump. Thank you for visiting <3

I grew up in the Singapore sun, my childhood smelling of chlorine and hot rain. We'd often family in Australia, which smelt like wood chips and old pillowcases, and family in China, where the smell of fried meat and smoke filled the air.

I liked to take pictures when we'd travel mostly in South East Asia. What a spiritual region.

I moved to New York during the pandemic in 2021. I was writing alot at NYU, for my journalism major and as a writing tutor.

Frickin psychotic city

We graduated in 2024. I stayed in New York another year, writing news at a luxury real estate publication.

In summer of 2025 I left the city, and spent time traveling listening to others stories…

…and getting clearer on my own.

In March of 2026, I came to Shanghai as a language student.

Thank you for making it all the way down here, and joining me in my journey of discovering my editorial voice, visual language and response to culture
